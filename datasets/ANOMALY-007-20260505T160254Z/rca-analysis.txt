### **Root Cause Analysis (RCA) for ANOMALY-007**

---

## **1. Affected Services**
The following services exhibit anomalies based on the observability data:

| **Service**       | **Anomaly Type**                          | **Severity** |
|-------------------|------------------------------------------|-------------|
| **HTTP Layer**    | Missing request rate & error metrics     | **Critical** |
| **All Services**  | Extreme metric compression (98-99%)      | **High**    |
| **Shipping**      | High trace volume (9670/10537 traces)    | **Medium**  |
| **Orders, Carts** | Elevated response times (avg 1.8-2.0ms)  | **Low**     |

**Key Observations:**
- **HTTP metrics (`http_requests_rate`, `http_errors`) are missing**, suggesting either:
  - A **metrics collection failure** (Prometheus scrape issue).
  - A **complete drop in HTTP traffic** (unlikely, given traces exist).
- **Extreme metric compression (98-99% reduction)** in `http_response_times`, `container_cpu`, and `service_health` indicates:
  - **Data aggregation/retention policies** are too aggressive.
  - **Prometheus storage or downsampling** is misconfigured.
- **Shipping service dominates traces** (92% of total), which may indicate:
  - A **spike in shipping-related requests** (e.g., batch processing, retries).
  - A **sampling bias** in tracing (e.g., only shipping traces are being captured).

---

## **2. Root Cause**
**Most Likely Cause:**
**Prometheus scrape failure or misconfiguration leading to missing HTTP metrics and extreme data compression.**

**Supporting Hypotheses:**
1. **Prometheus Scrape Target Down or Unreachable**
   - If Prometheus cannot scrape HTTP metrics (e.g., due to network issues, misconfigured `scrape_configs`, or a crashed exporter), `http_requests_rate` and `http_errors` would show **no data**.
   - The **98-99% metric compression** suggests Prometheus is **downsampling aggressively** (possibly due to storage pressure or retention policies).

2. **Prometheus Storage or Retention Issues**
   - If Prometheus is **low on disk space**, it may **drop or compress metrics** to conserve storage.
   - The **Douglas-Peucker algorithm** (used for compression) is applied **too aggressively**, losing granularity.

3. **Tracing Sampling Bias (Less Likely)**
   - The **shipping service dominates traces**, but this could be normal if it’s a high-throughput service.
   - However, if **other services are not being traced**, this could indicate a **sampling misconfiguration** in the tracing pipeline.

## **5. Suggested Fix**
### **Immediate Actions**
1. **Check Prometheus Scrape Status**
   - Run:
     ```sh
     curl -s http://<prometheus-server>/api/v1/targets | jq '.data.activeTargets[] | {health, lastError, scrapeUrl}'
     ```
   - **Expected:** All targets should be `health: "up"` with no `lastError`.
   - **If down:** Check:
     - **Network connectivity** between Prometheus and exporters.
     - **Exporter logs** (e.g., `journalctl -u prometheus-node-exporter`).
     - **Scrape configs** (`prometheus.yml`) for misconfigured targets.

2. **Verify Prometheus Storage & Retention**
   - Check disk usage:
     ```sh
     df -h /var/lib/prometheus
     ```
   - Check retention settings:
     ```sh
     grep -A5 "storage" /etc/prometheus/prometheus.yml
     ```
   - **If low on disk:** Increase retention or add storage.
   - **If compression is too aggressive:** Adjust `storage.tsdb.retention` or disable downsampling.

3. **Check Tracing Pipeline**
   - Verify **sampling rate** in Tempo/Jaeger:
     ```sh
     grep -A5 "sampler" /etc/tempo.yaml
     ```
   - If **shipping traces dominate**, check if:
     - There’s a **real spike in shipping requests** (e.g., batch job).
     - **Sampling is misconfigured** (e.g., only shipping traces are being captured).

### **Long-Term Remediation**
1. **Improve Prometheus Reliability**
   - **Set up alerts** for scrape failures (`up == 0`).
   - **Increase retention** (e.g., `storage.tsdb.retention.time: 30d`).
   - **Add more storage** if disk is full.

2. **Enhance Observability**
   - **Add synthetic monitoring** (e.g., Blackbox Exporter) to detect HTTP outages.
   - **Implement SLOs** for critical services (e.g., shipping, orders).

3. **Review Tracing Configuration**
   - **Adjust sampling** to ensure all services are traced fairly.
   - **Add trace-based alerts** (e.g., high latency in orders/carts).

4. **Post-Mortem & Documentation**
   - **Document the incident** (e.g., "Prometheus scrape failure due to misconfigured targets").
   - **Update runbooks** for similar issues.
